Why Ohana?

🌺 Ohana Shave Ice is bringing paradise to the mainland with the sweet flavor of shave ice 🍧🌴. We now have 100% Kona coffee ☕ and açaí bowls 🥣 available!

If you've ever visited the Hawaiian Islands 🏝️, you may have planned part or all of your days around acquiring your shave ice 🍧 — and returned to the mainland wishing you could’ve stayed longer or had just one more. Many families, like ours, came home with that same sunny feeling... 🌞💭

That feeling inspired the creation of Ohana Shave Ice 🌈. You don't have to save your shave ice for your next trip to Hawaii — we bring you real island flavors 🍍 and the best Hawaiian shave ice around 🍓🍧.

We also pride ourselves in making high-quality syrups 🍒 and only work with true Hawaiian concentrates 🌺 straight from the islands.

There’s a big difference between a sno-cone 🧊, shaved ice ❄️, and shave ice 🍧. Our shave ice is like eating light and fluffy snowflakes 🌨️ — it absorbs our flavorful syrups instead of letting them drain to the bottom like regular sno-cones.
